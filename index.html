About

I am an EECS undergraduate at UC Berkeley with interests in ASIC design, computer architecture, robotics, and control systems. My experience includes RISC-V CPU design, RTL development, PCB design, and robotics software using ROS.

Selected Experience & Projects

  • RISC-V CPU Design (ASIC) — Designed and verified a pipelined RISC-V processor in Verilog with hazard handling, CSR support, and cache integration.
  • Microfabrication Technology — Fabricated NMOS devices through a full cleanroom process flow and characterized devices using I–V and C–V measurements.
  • Robotics & Control — Implemented visual servoing and trajectory tracking for a 7-DOF Sawyer arm, and developed path planning and state estimation for TurtleBot.
  • PCB Design — Designed a custom multi-layer USB MIDI keyboard PCB using KiCad, focusing on power integrity and signal reliability.
